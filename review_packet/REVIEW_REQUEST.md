# Independent Review Request

I am seeking expert review of LTBA, an experimental guarded-local symbolic representation for computer algebra systems.

I am not claiming that LTBA replaces BDDs, Piecewise, or existing CAS systems.

The specific question is:

> Is LTBA a known representation under another name, technically flawed, or worth formalizing as a pre-Piecewise intermediate representation?

Requested review:

1. Check novelty against existing CAS / decision diagram / rewriting literature.
2. Check whether the benchmarks are fair.
3. Identify the strongest objections.
4. Recommend whether this should become a technical note, paper, or prototype contribution.
